About Me

Hello! I'm Rogi Auvi, I am a passionate and dedicated educator in the field of Software Engineering, currently teaching at SMKN 4 Payakumbuh. With a rich background and extensive experience in web development, Android application development, augmented reality (AR), and game design, I strive to inspire and mentor the next generation of tech innovators. As an educator, I believe in the power of hands-on learning and real-world applications. My classes are designed to not only impart technical knowledge but also to foster creativity, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills. I am committed to equipping my students with the tools and mindset they need to thrive in the ever-evolving landscape of technology. Over the years, I have had the privilege of working on numerous projects that span across various domains, from developing sleek and responsive websites to creating immersive AR experiences and engaging mobile apps. These projects not only reflect my technical expertise but also my passion for pushing the boundaries of what technology can achieve. When I'm not teaching or working on a project, you can find me exploring the latest trends in technology, attending workshops, and collaborating with fellow educators and industry professionals. My goal is to continuously learn and grow, so I can bring fresh and innovative ideas to my students and the tech community. Thank you for visiting my portfolio. Feel free to explore my projects and get in touch if you have any questions or would like to collaborate!
