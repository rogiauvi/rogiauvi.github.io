About Me

Hello! I'm Rogi Auvi, I am a passionate and dedicated educator in the field of Software Engineering, currently teaching at SMKN 4 Payakumbuh. With a rich background and extensive experience in web development, Android application development, augmented reality (AR), and game design, I strive to inspire and mentor the next generation of tech innovators. As an educator, I believe in the power of hands-on learning and real-world applications. My classes are designed to not only impart technical knowledge but also to foster creativity, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills. Over the years, I have had the privilege of working on numerous projects that span across various domains, from developing sleek and responsive websites to creating immersive AR experiences and engaging mobile apps.
